Classic Pancakes Recipe

These classic pancakes are soft, fluffy, and easy to make. They are perfect for breakfast and taste great with syrup, fruit, or butter.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix the flour, sugar, and baking powder together.
  2. In another bowl, whisk the milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Pour the wet mixture into the dry ingredients and stir until combined.
  4. Heat a pan over medium heat and pour in a small amount of batter.
  5. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook the other side until golden brown.
  6. Serve warm with your favorite toppings.
